A . P . J . Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He inspired millions with his vision, humility, and deep love for the youth of India. His life was a beacon of dedication, knowledge, and service to the nation.

Why I Admire Him

  • Dedicated his life to science and education
  • Remained humble despite great achievements
  • Inspired generations with his thoughts and books
